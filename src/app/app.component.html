About Us

iDurg Travel is built around meaningful travel, not rushed sightseeing.

iDurg Travel specializes in immersive Bhutan experiences designed to feel personal and welcoming. Every itinerary is thoughtfully customized to match your interests so you can explore the culture, discover hidden corners, and enjoy moments most travelers never get to see.

Founded by Deepanshu Sangwan and Anushia, iDurg Travel believes travel should change the way you see the world. Anushia, originally from Bhutan, brings a deep local perspective, while Deepanshu brings a global travel lens shaped by years of exploration.

Why Bhutan

Bhutan offers calm, culture, and a rare sense of stillness.

Gross National Happiness

Bhutan measures progress through wellbeing, not just growth, creating a gentler pace of travel and life.

Living Culture

From dzongs and monasteries to festivals and family-run stays, every corner feels rooted in tradition.

Untouched Landscapes

Himalayan views, pine forests, river valleys, and quiet mountain roads make every journey cinematic.
